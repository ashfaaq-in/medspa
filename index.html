Common Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our treatments, using the knowledge and expertise of our team.

Botox is an FDA-approved injectable treatment that uses a purified protein to temporarily relax facial muscles. By targeting the most common areas of movement on your face like your forehead, eyes, and mouth, Botox reduces the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines while preserving your natural expressions.

Juvederm is a family of FDA-approved hyaluronic acid fillers that restore lost volume, smooth lines, and enhance facial features for a naturally refreshed appearance. By replenishing the HA your skin naturally loses over time, results can last 12–24 months depending on the product and area treated.

Our microneedling technique of choice at Zona Med Spa is SkinPen. This minimally invasive procedure creates microscopic channels on your skin to stimulate collagen and elastin production for smoother, more youthful skin. This FDA-cleared device effectively reduces fine lines, acne scars, stretch marks, and texture issues.

HydraFacial is a patented multi-step facial that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates your skin for an instant glow and long-term improvements in texture and tone. This non-invasive procedure uses vortex technology and nourishing serums to address concerns like dryness, acne, fine lines, and rosacea — and is suitable for virtually all skin types.

Our BBL photofacial is non-invasive light therapy that uses broad band light to target sun damage, redness, and pigmentation for a clearer, more even complexion. This customizable procedure stimulates collagen, reduces broken capillaries and rosacea, and improves texture.

We believe in providing you with the knowledge you need to choose the treatment that will accomplish your individual goals. Book a consultation with our team and Michele will personally assess your skin, listen to your concerns, and recommend a personalized treatment plan tailored to you.
